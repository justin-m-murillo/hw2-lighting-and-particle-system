My scene is a general representation of an urban city/town. My inspiration for this scene comes from where I grew up in. My childhood home had many apartments, restaurants, shops, and a park with basketball courts across from where I lived. The trees and greenery in this scene juxtaposed with the human-made structures symbolize my city's attempt to improve the infrastructure and livelihood of our community without neglecting our environment. The main character of this scene is the blue car that is positioned between the stores and apartments. This car is a representation of me when I would drive through the city surrounded by structures and nature. Despite being a bustling community, driving around always felt relaxing as it gave me personal space to think about anything that was on my mind.
